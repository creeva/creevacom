Earlier I had written that Google really needs to integrate it’s reporting services<\/a> across the board. Now I still hope that do unify their reporting experience, but it seems Feedburner<\/a> is going to be the first to get sucked into the already fractured reporting structure that exists for Google<\/a>‘s many services.<\/p>\n

Earlier today on April 25 Feedburner posted that they were finally going to integrate their service with the standard google account logins<\/a>. This means the same account I login into Gmail<\/a>, Adsense<\/a>, etc, and etc. will work with Feedburner. Using this unification I’m hoping that we’ll be able to finally embed Adsense into our RSS feeds. I’m also hoping that the reporting (hint Google) can be blended in with Web Analytics<\/a>.<\/p>\n
