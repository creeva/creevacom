<\/p>\n

The Social Crosspost<\/strong><\/p>\n

by<\/p>\n

Creeva<\/p>\n

I’m am a social creature
\nOr that is how I seem
\nI’m active on all the networks
\nall in an elaborate scheme<\/p>\n

You see my updates on myspace
\nFlickr photos and my tweets
\nI just updated my blog
\nIt’s a tech article, not so sweet<\/p>\n

I talk about my family
\nI blog about my dog
\nMy cats eat cigarettes
\nOut back I chopped a log<\/p>\n

It seems that I spend all my time
\nPosting everywhere
\nReally it’s all automated
\nRSS taking thing here and there<\/p>\n

E-Mail sends to blogger
\nTwitter get’s a feed
\nFlickr is imported
\nRegurgitated as I need<\/p>\n

Tumblr is updated
\nPulls in it’s own stuff
\nPing.fm sends my status
\nAll filler and no fluff<\/p>\n

I’m not really all that social
\nI just type all day
\nI’m really anti-social
\nThat’s my path, my way<\/p>\n

I have all these friends on my list
\nMost I’ve never met
\nIRL they wouldn’t give me the time of day
\nThough they’ll comment on my pet<\/p>\n

My wife is driven bonkers
\nAbout my tweets everywhere
\nPownce updates her constantly
\nThat I just brushed my hair<\/p>\n

Dandelife categorizes me
\nTimelines my blog posts
\nIt doesn’t know
\nWhere I post the most<\/p>\n

Livejournal, Xanga, Mutiply
\nThey all get my writings
\nBut when people visit my home blog
\nThen my stats are exciting<\/p>\n

Why do I still do this
\nIs it attention that I crave
\nI crosspost and refeed my life
\nSo the data I can save<\/p>\n

Geekery and life
\nDon’t always co-exist
\nHang I’ll finish in a minute
\nRight after I tweet about this piss.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
