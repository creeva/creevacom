In my quest to beat every game officially released for the NES system – I started with 1942 the first game on the list.<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

Today was the first time I ever played 1942. Early in the day, I had to continue multiple times on the first level. Thank God this game has unlimited continues. This game is a top-down vertical scroller game. While I can get some enjoyment from these games, generally I loathe them over time and can’t play them for more than a few rounds.<\/p>\n

Seeing this was an arcade port I used an NES Advantage<\/a> to play through it. I didn’t use slow motion, auto-fire, or any cheat codes. While I will use the continues and count those I will beat every NES game fairly.<\/p>\n

Because you have to think to continue (if you hit start at Game Over it starts from the beginning and hence deletes your progress) I had to go through this multiple times, one time it was the dogs that caused this as they hit start for me in between rounds.<\/p>\n

I managed to complete the game with five continues for all 32 levels (3 were in the last 10). The highest score was the first round with a score of 451500 according to Twin Galaxies<\/a> this would qualify me for the third place spot for the high score<\/a> of all time (recorded). After things are all said and done I may come back to this game for a world record attempt later on. We’ll see how that goes.<\/p>\n

Ratings<\/span>:<\/p>\n

Graphics<\/span> -4\/10 – Graphics are not really the high point – but then the poor graphics don’t get in the way. The one sore point I have with the graphics is that sometimes the background made the enemies and the weapons shot at me very hard to see. If I can say anything that is the biggest flaw I can see with this game.<\/p>\n

Sound<\/span> – 2\/10 – Sound is really lacking – turn down the volume and listen to something else you would enjoy more<\/p>\n

Controls<\/span> 9\/10 – like any games of this type control is important and this game aims to please<\/p>\n

Story<\/span> – 2\/10 – the only story is you know this is a WWII pilot going through waves of fighters – since some games have less of a story than this – it gets a 2<\/p>\n

Ending – <\/span>1\/10 – It says congratulations – that’s it no credits or anything – and it only says congratulations for about 2 seconds before going to the game over screen.<\/p>\n

Fun<\/span> – 7\/10 – since I don’t really like this type of game normally and enjoyed myself I’m giving it higher than I normally would have.<\/p>\n
