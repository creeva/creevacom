While I already have a home network, looking at things to do with it online is a painful task. Unless I know what I am doing and have a topic to search on I can never seem to find anything new and interesting to do.<\/p>\n

This is why I am starting this blog. I am going through an overhaul of my home network. Still not done rewiring the house with CAT5 so I’ll be doing some interesting wireless tricks. I believe in centralized management, and we are going to try to make everything as transparent to the users on the network as possible.
