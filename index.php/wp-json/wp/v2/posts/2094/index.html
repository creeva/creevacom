This was my first Woolybear Festival since the year 2000, before I moved to Oregon. It was an experience and the first time that I ever heard of where the local band festival on a different weekend then the Woolybear.<\/p>\n

It was entertaining riding on the alumni float and playing away. I did manage to see an old friend from high school in the crowd. Ironically my ex-girlfriend was standing next to him trying not to be seen (she wasn’t hiding from me per say but the entire alumni band since I heard she had a falling out a few years earlier).<\/p>\n

I did manage to yell at a few people who I knew in the crowd for not coming up and joining the band since they had marched in high school.<\/p>\n
