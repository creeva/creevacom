One thing I’ve wanted to do is upload images from my Nokia N810 to my Flickr Account<\/a>. I’ve mucked with mailing photos, but that’s never fun with bulk amount of images. You might think that because the N810 doesn’t have a normal sd slot, going for the micro-sd instead that I wouldn’t have a lot of photos on here. Ironically whenever I take photos on my RAZR cell phone the only way I can get them off is via my N810, in which it works right away.<\/p>\n

Before you state that I can purchase or pirate Motorola Phone Tools<\/a> – why should I when I have my N810 \ud83d\ude1b<\/p>\n

I played with a progam called Maemo Publishr<\/a>, but that was slow and clunky, and in the end I still was not able to get any pictures actually uploaded. Doing a bit more research it seems a program I was already using, Maemo Wordpy<\/a>, could already do this. I authorized it Flickr to accept photos from Maemo Wordpy and I was off to the races. In under a minute I had all forty-three images off of my cell phone and in my Flickr account.<\/p>\n

If you want to upload to Flickr<\/a> from your N810 – Maemo Wordpy is the key.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
