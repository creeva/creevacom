\n

From your 76 subscriptions<\/strong>, over the last 30 days you read 14,054 items<\/strong>, starred 177 items<\/strong>, shared 41 items<\/strong>, and emailed 39 items<\/strong>.<\/p>\n<\/blockquote>\n

That is 14,054 articles that I skimmed in the last 30 days, The starred items are things that I want to get back and read later. So if we take the averages:<\/p>\n

Each day I read (or at least skim) 469 articles<\/p>\n

Each day I star 6 articles (which is a misnomer since an hour later I unstar them, so it only counts currently starred items I think)<\/p>\n

Each day I share 1-2 articles<\/a><\/p>\n

Each day I email 1-2 articles<\/p>\n

That is literally just what I get from RSS – let’s be fair and say I go to 50-100 websites a day reading an untold number of articles. I have no true way of tracking that since I do not capture that data at all.<\/p>\n
