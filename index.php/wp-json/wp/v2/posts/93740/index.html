The first time I heard this song I was half asleep. It was sometime in my senior of high school and I always fell asleep playing the radio all night. Normally I didn’t wake up in the night, but this song seemed to have just the right sound of being different that I did wake up. I thought it was awesome and went to school the next day to find someone else heard this song. No one had heard it yet. I thought I had dreamt it and thought that was crazy. <\/p>\n

That first time must have been the very first time that WWMS had played the song. A couple weeks later the song was everywhere and everyone had heard it. There aren’t many times I can say that I remember the exact moment I heard a particular song, but this was one of those times. <\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
