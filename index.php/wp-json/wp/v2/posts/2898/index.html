I’m a Twitter addict. I’m not going to be apologetic about it. I love twitter<\/a>. I’ll be happier when the rewrite occurs and it becomes a stable messaging platform. The problem I have is balancing the number of tweets I do with the number of my real-life friends are willing to read.<\/p>\n

I really want to tweet every song I’m listening to, every image I upload, and every piece of digital property I touch and interact with. I don’t though since that could generate hundreds of tweets on some days. My friends won’t stand for it. So I use things like a filtered RSS feed to alert my followers of new blog posts, and I actually do interact with it (unlike some services).<\/p>\n

I’m not asking for a cookie, I’m just having a random rant.<\/p>\n
