I saw Casino Royale last night at the AMC theater on Times Square. I have to say the actor playing Bond is great – and awful at the same time. The reason he is great is since this is an inception story it shows all the problems and ego that would go with a character who was bond-like but not James Bond. By the end of the movie you see him change and be formed – and that is worthy of the movie.<\/p>\n
