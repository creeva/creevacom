Cross-posting to my livejournal<\/a> account has been fixed. This was important to me since going from here, to livejournal<\/a>, dandelife<\/a> pulls in the information for my timeline widget there. It’s all small and pretty much a petty thing, but it’s part of my broadcasting to friends on other social networks. I still need to figure out why my crossposting to my MSN space isn’t working. But all my other cross-posting functions are working.<\/p>\n

To fix this is issue the WordPress livejournal crosspost plugin<\/a>. I searched google and came across the blog “control, shift, cheesburge<\/a>r<\/a>” where he had also had the issue after upgrading to WordPress 2.5. He was discussing in one of his posts<\/a> which plugins he had working and what wasn’t, he mentioned that he had to adjust the livejournal crosspost plugin by modifying a couple of lines. The only problem? He didn’t specify which lines. I posted a comment asking which he modified and thankfully he answered me. <\/span><\/p>\n

He stated he managed to get the information from a google group about the plugin<\/a>. The line you need to adjust is the lines<\/p>\n

if(version_compare($wp_version, \"2.1\", \"<\")) {
\nrequire_once(ABSPATH . '\/wp-includes\/template-functions-links.php');
\n}<\/code>
\nNeed to be changed to:<\/p>\n

if(version_compare($wp_version, \"2.3\", \"<\")) {
\nrequire_once(ABSPATH . '\/wp-includes\/link-template.php');
\n}<\/code><\/p>\n
